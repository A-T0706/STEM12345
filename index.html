AI in Medicine

Using Artificial Intelligence to Detect Cancer Early

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology that allows computers to learn patterns from data and make predictions. In medicine, AI can analyze thousands of medical images faster than a doctor.

How AI Detects Cancer

  • Analyzes X-rays and MRIs
  • Finds tumors doctors might miss
  • Predicts cancer risk
  • Helps doctors make faster diagnoses

Future Impact

AI may allow doctors to detect cancer years earlier, improving survival rates and saving lives.
